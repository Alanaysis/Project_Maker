# Query Parser Examples
# =====================

# Simple term search
hello

# Boolean AND (explicit)
hello AND world

# Boolean AND (implicit)
hello world

# Boolean OR
hello OR world

# Boolean NOT
NOT goodbye

# Phrase query
"hello world"

# Grouping with parentheses
(hello OR hi) AND world

# Complex boolean
(quick OR fast) AND "brown fox" NOT lazy

# Nested parentheses
((a AND b) OR (c AND d)) AND e

# Mixed operators
apple AND (banana OR cherry) NOT date

# Only NOT
NOT (spam OR junk)

# Phrase with boolean
"machine learning" AND (python OR tensorflow)
